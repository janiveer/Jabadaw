Jabadaw Tunebook

The Jabadaw Tunebook is available now. It features around 90 new tunes written by past and present members of the band — mostly dance tunes, but with a few weird and wonderful concert pieces included also. It costs £11.00 including post and packaging.

The tunes are suitable for most chromatic melody instruments. Over half of them have guitar chords and / or harmony parts, and two or three have full-blown arrangements for melody instrument and piano. The book also includes a chord chart and background notes on the tunes.

You can download a sample from the Jabadaw Tunebook here — includes one chapter and the complete index of tune titles.
